Recruitment Process

Sanskriti consultancy follows the 6 steps to hire the best suitable candidate for the client.
These Steps are mention below:

• Understand the Requirement of Client.
• Find the Suitable CV From different techniques.
• Take the primary interview of candidate.
• Arrange face 2 face interview of potential hire with client.
• Finalize the recruitment process.

We give the full assistance to the company in determining their manpower needs through determining
the qualifications, competencies, experience sought in the incumbent; We are fully responsible for the
entire recruitment process inclusive of sourcing for applicable candidates utilizing our database or
advertising using different media options; We are responsible for screening of the proposed candidates
and providing our clients with a shortlist of most suitable candidates as well as participating in the final
selection and hiring process.
